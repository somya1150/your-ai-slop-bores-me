Privacy Policy

The short version

Your AI Slop Bores Me operates without any accounts and with near anonymity. We store your IP address if you are restricted. We also do not preserve any unreported/flagged content for more than an hour.

What we collect

Device fingerprint: Generated and stored locally in your browser. Used to keep your sessions consistent and enforce moderation.

IP address: Used for rate limiting and ban enforcement.

User ID: A random ID generated in your browser and stored locally. Used for credits and game state.

User-submitted content: Prompts, text responses, and drawings you send through the game.

How we use it

To run the game: delivering prompts and responses, tracking credits, moderating content, and rate limiting.

Third-Party Services

We use self-host fonts, but use Google Fonts as a fallback for font delivery, and Cloudflare Turnstile for bot/spam protection. Turnstile sets cookies in your browser. No user content or identifying information is shared with any third-party service.

Data Retention

We do not retain most game data for more than an hour. We preserve prompts or responses only if reported and/or flagged by our system. We retain the IP addresses and fingerprints of restricted users indefinitely to prevent further abuse.

Children

The site is not intended for anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the site, contact us and we will take appropriate action.
