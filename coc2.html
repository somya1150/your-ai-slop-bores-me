Code of Conduct

This site only works if people keep it 
playable. These rules apply to prompts, 
responses, drawings, usernames, and any 
other content you send here.

Treat other people like people. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or targeted cruelty are not allowed.

Do not post threats, doxxing, self-harm instructions, explicit sexual content (even in contexts like song lyrics, as the site is for ages 13+), or anything meant to seriously harm someone.

No spam, scams, off-platform contact requests, or links. That includes invite links, usernames, and "hit me up" style messages.

Keep prompts and responses usable for the game. Repeated junk, abuse, or attempts to dodge moderation can get you restricted

Report stuff that crosses the line. We would rather review a bad post than leave it up

Violations may lead to content 
    removal,temporary restrictions,
or bans.
